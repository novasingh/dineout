Purpose: collect usability and acceptance metrics from end users (minimum 30 respondents). Use a 5-point Likert scale where 1=Strongly Disagree, 5=Strongly Agree.

Questions:
Q1. The app was easy to navigate and find menu items.
Q2. Adding items to cart and adjusting quantities was straightforward.
Q3. The order status and notifications were clear and timely.
Q4. The billing and payment process was easy to understand.
Q5. The app performed responsively (no noticeable lag) during my use.
Q6. I would recommend this app for in-restaurant ordering.

Demographics: age bracket, role (customer/staff/admin), device type (phone/tablet), any comments.

Sampling plan and instructions:
- Recruit at least 30 respondents representing customers and staff.
- Provide the test scenario: place an order, monitor status, and complete payment (or review billing screen).
- Collect answers in a CSV with columns: RespondentID,AgeBracket,Role,DeviceType,Q1..Q6,Comments
